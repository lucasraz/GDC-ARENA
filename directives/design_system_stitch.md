## Creative North Star
"The Navigator’s Manuscript": A blend of historical archive gravitas and modern football journalism energy.

## Typography
- **Headlines (Display/Headline):** Epilogue (Aggressive, bold, tight kerning).
- **Narrative (Body/Titles):** Newsreader (Serif, literary, high readability).
- **Labels/Technical:** Inter (Precise, functional).

## Core Rules
1.  **No-Line Rule:** PROHIBITED: 1px solid borders for sectioning. Use background color shifts (`surface` vs `surface-container-low`) to define boundaries.
2.  **Elevation:** Ambient depth via tonal layering. Stack lighter surfaces on darker ones for "lift".
3.  **Glassmorphism:** Use `surface-variant` at 70% opacity with `24px` backdrop blur for floating navigation.
4.  **Rhythm:** Strict 4px baseline grid.
5.  **Watermarks:** Maltese Cross as a low-contrast background element.
